The world's largest penguin colony lies on Zavodovski Island, where there are close to a million pairs of Chinstrap penguins.

To find food, the mother or father penguin must first set out towards the sea.

After a long journey, she reaches the cliffs overlooking the sea, and jumps in!

At last, it is time to hunt for some tasty fish!
